About Us

Chartwell Partners is a retained executive search firm focused on identifying and recruiting senior leadership for our clients. We work with a select number of clients to ensure a high level of service and successful outcomes. Our approach is bespoke, thorough, and confidential, leading to lasting placements that drive organizational success.

With decades of combined experience, our partners have a deep understanding of various industries and functional roles. We pride ourselves on our rigorous research, extensive network, and commitment to diversity and inclusion in every search.

Our Approach

Our methodology is designed to deliver exceptional results. We begin by gaining a comprehensive understanding of your organization's culture, strategic objectives, and the specific requirements of the role. This deep dive ensures we target candidates who not only possess the necessary skills and experience but also align with your company's vision.

We leverage a combination of proprietary research, industry insights, and a vast network to identify a diverse pool of highly qualified candidates. Our robust assessment process includes in-depth interviews, comprehensive background checks, and detailed referencing to ensure the best possible fit.

Core Values

Our operations are guided by a set of core values that define who we are and how we interact with clients and candidates:

  • Integrity: We operate with the highest ethical standards, ensuring transparency and trust in all our dealings.
  • Excellence: We are committed to delivering superior results through meticulous research and a rigorous selection process.
  • Partnership: We build long-term relationships based on mutual respect and a shared understanding of goals.
  • Confidentiality: We handle all assignments with the utmost discretion, protecting the privacy of both clients and candidates.
  • Innovation: We continuously adapt our strategies to market changes and client needs, ensuring cutting-edge solutions.
